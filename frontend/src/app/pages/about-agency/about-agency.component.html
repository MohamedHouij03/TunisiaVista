About TunisiaVista

Tunisia's premier travel agency since 2009.

Our Story

Born From a Love of Tunisia

TunisiaVista was founded in 2009 by Khaled Ben Youssef, a passionate Tunisian guide frustrated that most agencies presented Tunisia as a beach-only destination. He set out to create an agency that reveals the full depth of Tunisian culture and landscape.

Today, TunisiaVista is recognized as one of Tunisia's most respected travel companies, with partnerships with the National Tourism Board, UNESCO, and leading luxury hotels across the country.
